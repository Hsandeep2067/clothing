Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days, express delivery takes 1-2 business days, and same-day delivery is available in Colombo for orders placed before 12:00 PM.

Do you deliver on weekends?

Yes, we deliver on Saturdays but not on Sundays and public holidays. Saturday deliveries are available for express and standard shipping options.

What if I'm not home during delivery?

Our delivery partner will attempt delivery twice. If you're not available, they'll leave a delivery note with instructions for pickup or rescheduling.

Can I change my delivery address?

You can change your delivery address within 2 hours of placing your order by contacting our customer service team.

Is delivery free?

Standard delivery is free for orders above Rs. 2000. For orders below Rs. 2000, a delivery fee of Rs. 300 applies. Express and same-day delivery have additional charges.
